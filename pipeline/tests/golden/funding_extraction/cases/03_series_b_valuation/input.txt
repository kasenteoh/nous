Helioform lands $40M Series B at a $400M valuation

June 3, 2026 — Solar-software company Helioform has closed a $40 million Series B led
by Big Bend Growth, with participation from Ridgepath Capital. The round values the
company at $400 million post-money, according to a person familiar with the deal who
spoke to TechCrunch.

Helioform's platform designs residential solar layouts and automates permitting
paperwork for installers. Revenue has tripled year over year, the company said.

Big Bend Growth partner Maria Duran called the permitting workflow "the wedge into a
much bigger energy-software market."
