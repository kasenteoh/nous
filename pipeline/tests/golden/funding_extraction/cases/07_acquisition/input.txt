Palo Alto Networks acquires Saltmarsh Security for $410M

Palo Alto Networks said Monday it has completed its acquisition of Saltmarsh Security,
the cloud-permissions startup, in a deal valued at $410 million. Saltmarsh's
26-person team will join Palo Alto's cloud security group, and its product will be
folded into the Prisma line over the next year.

Saltmarsh Security, founded in 2021, sold software that maps and trims excess
permissions across cloud accounts. The company had raised venture funding from several
security-focused firms before the sale.

Terms suggest a strong outcome for the company's early backers.
