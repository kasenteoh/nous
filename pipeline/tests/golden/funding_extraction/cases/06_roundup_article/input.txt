This week in venture: five deals to know

A quick tour of the week's funding activity across the startup landscape.

Saber Analytics closed a $25M Series B led by Windward Capital to grow its retail
analytics suite.

Gullwing raised a $7M seed led by Foxtail Capital for its drone-inspection software.

Medlock Health added $12M in an extension round as it expands into three new states.

Pinebook, the note-taking app, raised $4M from a syndicate of consumer angels.

Corvid Systems landed $31M for warehouse robotics, led by Ironbark Ventures.

That's all for this week — see you next Friday.
