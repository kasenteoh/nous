Foundry Metrics adds $8M Series A extension

Foundry Metrics, which makes yield-analytics software for semiconductor fabs, has
closed an $8 million Series A extension led by returning investor Gatehouse Capital.
The company raised its original Series A in 2024.

The extension will fund deployments with two new fab customers in Arizona and a small
Tokyo office to support Japanese manufacturers.

Foundry Metrics says its software recovers one to two points of yield within the first
six months of deployment, by correlating inline sensor data with end-of-line test
results.
