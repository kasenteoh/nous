Corestone raises $18M Series A to bring spend controls to mid-market finance teams

March 12, 2026 — Corestone, the spend management platform for mid-market companies,
announced today that it has raised an $18 million Series A led by Meridian Ventures,
with participation from Foxglove Capital and Northgate Partners.

The company says the new capital will fund engineering hires and an expansion of its
accounts-payable automation product. Corestone launched its platform in 2024 and now
serves several hundred finance teams.

"Mid-market CFOs have been stuck between spreadsheets and bloated enterprise suites,"
said CEO Rachel Kim. "This round lets us close that gap."

Meridian Ventures general partner Alan Osei will join Corestone's board as part of the
financing.
