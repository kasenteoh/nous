Thornfield AI raises $60M to expand its sales-forecasting platform

Thornfield AI, the sales-forecasting company, announced a $60 million Series C led by
Crestview Partners on Tuesday. The company plans to expand into Europe and double its
go-to-market team.

Thornfield competes with a crowded field of revenue-intelligence vendors, including
Bramble and several larger incumbents, in a market that has consolidated rapidly over
the past two years.

Founded in 2020, Thornfield says it now counts more than 400 customers.
