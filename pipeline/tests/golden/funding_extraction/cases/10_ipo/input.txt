Lumenpath shares jump 24% in Nasdaq debut

Shares of Lumenpath, the network-observability company, rose 24 percent in their first
day of trading on the Nasdaq on Thursday, after the company completed its initial
public offering at $21 per share. The IPO raised approximately $310 million for the
company, which trades under the ticker LMNP.

Lumenpath was founded in 2016 and turned its first annual profit last year. Proceeds
will fund international expansion and continued investment in its sensor network.

The listing is the third software IPO this quarter, in what bankers describe as a
reopening window for venture-backed offerings.
