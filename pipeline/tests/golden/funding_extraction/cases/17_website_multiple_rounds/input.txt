Northbeam Data — About us

Northbeam Data builds a semantic layer that lets analysts define metrics once and use
them everywhere: dashboards, notebooks, and AI assistants all read from the same
definitions.

Company milestones

June 2023 — Founded in Portland, OR; raised a $4 million seed round from Cascade
Ventures and angels.

February 2024 — Launched general availability; first 100 customers.

September 10, 2025 — Announced our $15 million Series A led by Craft Ventures to scale
the platform.

January 2026 — Passed 400 customers and 2 billion daily metric queries.
