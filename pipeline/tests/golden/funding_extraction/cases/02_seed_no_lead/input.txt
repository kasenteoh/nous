Plumeria banks $3.5M seed round for its returns-management software

Plumeria, a startup building returns-management software for independent e-commerce
brands, has raised $3.5 million in seed funding. The round included Cindercone
Ventures, Harbor Street Capital, and a group of angel investors from the logistics
industry. The company declined to say who led the round.

Plumeria's software consolidates return requests, prints labels, and restocks
inventory across storefronts. The company says brands using the product process
returns twice as fast.

The startup plans to use the funding to double its six-person engineering team this
year.
