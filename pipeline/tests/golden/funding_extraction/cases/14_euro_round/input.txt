Stockholm's Bruket raises EUR 20M Series A for its industrial-maintenance platform

Bruket, a Stockholm-based startup whose software schedules preventive maintenance for
factories, has raised a EUR 20 million Series A led by Nordkap Ventures, with
participation from Baltia Industrial Partners.

The company will use the funding to expand across the Nordics and Germany. Bruket's
platform predicts equipment failures from sensor data and books technicians
automatically.

Founded in 2022, Bruket employs 45 people and counts several large manufacturers among
its customers.
