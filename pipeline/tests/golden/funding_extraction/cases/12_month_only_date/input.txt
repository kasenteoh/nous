Copperseed's quiet $12M Series A signals investor interest in farm robotics

In March 2026, Copperseed closed a $12 million Series A led by Prairie Gate Ventures,
the company confirmed this week. The agricultural-robotics startup had not previously
announced the round.

Copperseed builds autonomous weeding robots for row crops. The company says its
machines covered 40,000 acres last season and cut herbicide use sharply for
participating farms.

The round is among a string of recent bets on automation in agriculture.
