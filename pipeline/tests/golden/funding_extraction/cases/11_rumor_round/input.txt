Arclight said to be in talks to raise at a $5B valuation

Arclight, the code-generation startup, is in advanced talks with investors to raise a
new round that could value the company at as much as $5 billion, according to three
people familiar with the discussions. The deal has not closed, and the terms could
still change.

The company declined to comment. Investors approached for the potential round include
several existing backers.

A deal at that price would roughly triple the valuation Arclight commanded a year ago,
underscoring continued investor appetite for AI coding tools.
