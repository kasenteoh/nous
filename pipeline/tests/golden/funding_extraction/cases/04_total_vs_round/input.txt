Quillwork raises $30M Series A for AI contract review

January 22, 2026 — Quillwork, whose software reviews commercial contracts for legal
teams, said Thursday it has raised a $30 million Series A led by Larchmont Capital,
with participation from earlier backer Souter Ventures. The financing brings the
company's total raised to $45 million since its founding in 2023.

Quillwork's models flag non-standard clauses and draft fallback language inside the
tools lawyers already use. The company says review time drops by more than half for
its median customer.
