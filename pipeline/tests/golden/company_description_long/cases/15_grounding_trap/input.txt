Relaymark — Customer messaging on autopilot

Relaymark automates lifecycle messaging for e-commerce brands: welcome
series, abandoned-cart recovery, win-back campaigns, and post-purchase
flows across email and SMS.

How it works

Relaymark connects to Shopify and BigCommerce, builds a profile of every
shopper from orders and browsing events, and runs journeys from a visual
builder. Send-time optimization picks the hour each shopper is most likely
to engage, and a holdout system measures true incremental revenue per flow
rather than last-click attribution.

Templates and testing

Over 900 brands run on Relaymark. Journey templates encode what works —
the site cites a 4-message abandoned-cart series as its most-copied
template — and every flow can A/B test subject lines, timing, and offers.

Deliverability

Dedicated sending infrastructure, list hygiene tooling, and automatic
suppression of unengaged addresses keep sender reputation healthy.

Pricing scales with contactable profiles; email and SMS are metered
separately. A free tier covers stores under 500 profiles.

About

Relaymark is a 30-person company founded in 2020, fully remote, with
customers concentrated in fashion, beauty, and food & beverage.

Integrations

Beyond Shopify and BigCommerce, Relaymark syncs audiences to Meta and Google ad platforms for suppression and lookalikes, and webhooks stream every message event to your warehouse. Review-request flows integrate with Okendo and Judge.me.

Support

Every plan includes migration help: Relaymark's team rebuilds your existing flows during onboarding, typically inside two weeks, and a deliverability specialist reviews your first month of sends.
