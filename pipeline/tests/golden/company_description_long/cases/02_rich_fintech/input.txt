Ledgerline — Close the books without the spreadsheet marathon

Ledgerline automates payment reconciliation for finance teams. It pulls
transactions from Stripe, Adyen, PayPal, and your bank feeds, matches them
against invoices and ledger entries, and flags every discrepancy with a
suggested resolution.

The problem

For companies that sell across several payment providers, reconciliation is
a monthly spreadsheet marathon: exports from each processor, VLOOKUPs,
manual matching of fees, refunds, and chargebacks. Teams routinely spend the
first week of every month closing the previous one.

How Ledgerline works

Connect your payment processors and bank accounts through read-only
integrations. Ledgerline's matching engine pairs each payout with its
underlying transactions, nets out processor fees, currency conversion, and
refunds, and posts journal entries to NetSuite, QuickBooks, or Xero. Rules
you accept once are applied automatically the next month. Anything the
engine cannot match with confidence lands in a review queue with the
evidence attached.

Results our customers report

Finance teams at over 400 companies use Ledgerline. Customers report closing
the books in hours instead of days, and month-end close times cut by 70
percent. Ledgerline is SOC 2 Type II certified and never holds funds.

Pricing

Usage-based: a flat platform fee plus a per-transaction rate that declines
with volume. No implementation fee for standard integrations.
