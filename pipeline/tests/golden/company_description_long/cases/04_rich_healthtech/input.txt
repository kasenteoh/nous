Clinicore — Prior authorizations, off your plate

Clinicore automates prior authorization for specialty clinics. Our platform
reads the order from your EHR, assembles the payer-specific documentation,
submits it electronically, and tracks every request to a decision.

The problem we solve

Prior authorization is the top administrative burden in specialty care.
Staff spend hours on payer portals and phone calls; patients wait days for
approvals; denials arrive for missing documentation that was sitting in the
chart all along.

How it works

Clinicore integrates with Epic, Cerner, and athenahealth. When a clinician
places an order that needs authorization, Clinicore detects it, pulls the
clinical notes and labs that the payer's policy requires, and assembles the
submission. A rules engine encodes payer policies and keeps them current;
our clinical operations team reviews edge cases before anything goes out.
Status updates flow back into the EHR, and staff see one queue instead of
a dozen portals.

Results

Clinics using Clinicore report authorizations submitted in minutes instead
of hours, first-pass approval rates above 90 percent, and staff time on
authorizations cut by more than half.

Who we serve

Specialty practices and clinic groups in oncology, cardiology, rheumatology,
and imaging — from single-site clinics to groups with 40 locations.

Pricing is per provider per month, with volume pricing for groups.

About

Clinicore was founded in 2020 in Nashville, TN by a practice administrator
