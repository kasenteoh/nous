Briefhound — Contract review at in-house speed

Briefhound reviews commercial contracts for in-house legal teams. Upload an
NDA, MSA, or DPA and Briefhound redlines it against your playbook in
minutes, in tracked changes, inside Microsoft Word.

The problem

In-house teams are buried in routine paper. Sales waits days for an NDA;
legal burns evenings on low-risk redlines or ships them to outside counsel
at hundreds of dollars an hour.

How it works

Your playbook — preferred positions, fallback language, walk-away terms —
is encoded once, with a library of standard positions to start from.
Briefhound's language models classify each clause, compare it to the
playbook, and propose redlines with an explanation attached to every edit.
Lawyers review in Microsoft Word with tracked changes, accept or adjust,
and the playbook learns from what they change. An approvals dashboard shows
cycle times and where negotiations stall.

What customers see

Legal teams report first-pass review in under 10 minutes and NDA turnaround
cut from days to same-day. Briefhound handles NDAs, MSAs, DPAs, order
forms, and vendor paper.

Security: SOC 2 Type II, customer data never used to train shared models.

Pricing: per-seat annual subscription with unlimited documents.

About

Briefhound was founded in Boston in 2021 by a former in-house counsel and
two NLP engineers. 44 people. Compared to outside counsel, Briefhound
positions routine review as software: the lawyer stays the decision-maker,
the drafting grunt work moves to the machine.
