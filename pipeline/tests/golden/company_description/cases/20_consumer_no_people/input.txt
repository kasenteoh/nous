Loopnote — A journal that writes back

Loopnote is a private journaling app that turns your entries into gentle weekly
reflections. Write freely; Loopnote notices themes, moods, and habits, and hands you a
Sunday summary of the week you actually had.

Private by design

Entries are end-to-end encrypted. No ads, no feeds, no social graph. Your journal is
yours — export everything, anytime.

How people use it

Morning pages before work. Decision logs. Gratitude lists. Therapy prep. Loopnote
adapts to whatever you write.

Free to try for 30 days, then $4 a month. Available on iOS and Android.
