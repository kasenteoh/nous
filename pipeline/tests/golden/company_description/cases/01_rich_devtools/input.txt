Fluxline — CI observability for fast-moving teams

Fluxline gives engineering teams a single view of every build, test, and deploy.
Our platform ingests events from GitHub Actions, CircleCI, and Buildkite, then
surfaces flaky tests, slow pipelines, and regressions before they block a release.

How it works

Connect your CI provider in under five minutes. Fluxline's collectors stream build
telemetry into a queryable timeline: every job, every retry, every artifact. Our
flake detector replays failing tests in isolation and flags the ones that fail
nondeterministically, so your team stops re-running whole suites to get a green check.
Dashboards break down pipeline time by stage, repository, and team, and budget alerts
fire when a merge queue starts to back up.

Who uses Fluxline

Platform teams at companies with 50 to 5,000 engineers use Fluxline to cut CI spend
and keep merge queues moving. Customers typically reduce time-to-green by a third
within the first month.
