Farepoint — Faster payouts for marketplaces

Farepoint is the payout infrastructure for online marketplaces. One API to onboard
sellers, run compliance checks, and send funds to 40+ currencies with same-day
settlement in major corridors.

Built for platforms

Marketplaces use Farepoint to pay sellers, creators, and gig workers without building
a payments team. Our dashboard handles KYC, tax forms, and reconciliation; our API
handles the money movement. Volume-based pricing with no monthly minimums.

Trusted rails

Farepoint is an FCA-authorised payment institution. Client funds are safeguarded in
segregated accounts, and every payout carries a full audit trail.
