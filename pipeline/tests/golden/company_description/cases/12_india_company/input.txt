Kaveri Cloud — Cut your cloud bill, keep your speed

Kaveri Cloud helps engineering teams reduce cloud spend without slowing down. Our
platform connects to your AWS, GCP, or Azure accounts, builds a live map of what every
service costs, and recommends rightsizing, scheduling, and commitment purchases that
typically cut bills by 25-40%.

Autopilot savings

Turn on Autopilot and Kaveri Cloud executes approved optimizations for you: idle
resources are parked on nights and weekends, oversized instances are stepped down
gradually, and savings plans are purchased against forecasted usage.

Engineering-first

Unlike finance-oriented tools, Kaveri Cloud speaks in services and deployments, not
just invoices. Slack alerts tell the owning team when a deploy doubles a service's
burn rate.
