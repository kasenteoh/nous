Harborlight Health — Care coordination software for community clinics

Harborlight Health helps community health centers coordinate patient care across
visits, referrals, and follow-ups. Our platform gives care teams a shared worklist,
closes referral loops with two-way faxing and e-referrals, and tracks quality measures
for UDS reporting.

Built for FQHCs

Federally qualified health centers run on thin margins and complex reporting
requirements. Harborlight integrates with the EHRs clinics already use, flags missed
follow-ups, and drafts the outreach lists staff work through each morning.

Outcomes

Clinics using Harborlight report fewer missed referrals and stronger quality-measure
performance within two quarters.

Security

HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with SOC 2 Type II certification. PHI is encrypted in
transit and at rest.
