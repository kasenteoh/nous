Shipstream — freight visibility software for modern logistics teams

Shipstream tracks every shipment across ocean, air, and road in one dashboard. We pull
live signals from carriers, ports, and telematics providers, normalize them, and give
operations teams accurate ETAs with automated exception alerts when a container slips.

Why teams choose Shipstream

Predictive ETAs: our models flag delays days before the carrier updates its status.
Exception workflows: route a late shipment to the right owner automatically.
Customer portal: give shippers a live tracking page without email back-and-forth.
