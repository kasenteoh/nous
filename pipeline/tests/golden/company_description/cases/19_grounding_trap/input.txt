Meridian Ops — Incident response that runs itself

Meridian Ops is an incident management platform for engineering teams. When an alert
fires, Meridian assembles the responders, opens a channel in Slack, pulls in the
relevant runbook, and drafts the status-page update — before a human has typed a word.

From alert to retro

Meridian integrates with PagerDuty and Opsgenie for paging, captures the full incident
timeline automatically, and generates a draft postmortem with contributing factors and
action items. Action items sync to Jira and are tracked to completion.

Trusted by more than 2,000 engineering teams.

Founded in 2020. Remote-first, with a hub office in Austin, TX.
