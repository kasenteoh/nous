Crateful — Wholesale food sourcing, simplified

Crateful is a wholesale marketplace connecting independent restaurants with local
farms and specialty food producers. Chefs browse live inventory, order from multiple
suppliers in one cart, and get consolidated next-day delivery.

For restaurants

Compare prices across suppliers, set par levels, and reorder in two taps. Invoices
land in one dashboard that syncs with your accounting software.

For producers

Reach hundreds of restaurant buyers without hiring a sales team. Crateful handles
payments, logistics, and returns; you set the price.

Now live in Chicago, IL and expanding to two more cities this year. Founded by
restaurant-industry veterans in 2022.
