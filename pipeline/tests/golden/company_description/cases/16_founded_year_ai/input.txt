Tensorforge AI — Fine-tune and serve open models on your own terms

Tensorforge AI is a platform for training, fine-tuning, and serving open-weight
language models. Bring your data, pick a base model, and get a deployed endpoint with
autoscaling, observability, and cost controls.

Own your models

Everything runs in your cloud account or ours. Weights, adapters, and training data
never leave your control, and every run is reproducible from a config file.

Performance without the platform team

Managed clusters schedule training across spot GPUs to cut costs; inference servers
batch and cache automatically. Teams report serving costs dropping by half versus
generic GPU rentals.

Founded in 2022, Tensorforge AI is headquartered in Seattle, WA and serves customers
in healthcare, finance, and developer tooling.
